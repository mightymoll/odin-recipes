Lasagna

Garfield's Famous Lasagna

Lasagna so good that Jon won't care how fat it makes him!

This recipe has no twists, it's just a classic take on everybody's favorite Italian dish. Continue reading for the ingredient list and recipe.

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • noodles
  • ground beef
  • spices

Steps

  1. cook noodles
  2. layer noodles with sauce and ground beef
  3. add a LOT of cheese
  4. place in oven and bake at 350 for 45 minutes
