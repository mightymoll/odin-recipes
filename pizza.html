Garfield's Perfect Pizza

Pizza so good that Jon won't care how fat it makes him!

This recipe has no twists, it's just a classic take on everybody's favorite bread and cheese combo.

See the ingredient list and recipe below.

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • cheese
  • pepperoni
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. flatten dough into a circle
  2. spread tomato sauce over the dough with a spoon
  3. add a LOT of cheese
  4. add pepperoni‘s in a fun smiley face
  5. place in oven and bake at 350 for 45 minutes
